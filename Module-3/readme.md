## Company Under Review

**Adani Ports and Special Economic Zone Limited (APSEZ)**

---

## 1️⃣ Valuation Score — **6.5 / 10**

### What works

* **Stock P/E: 27.4** → reasonable for infrastructure with scale + visibility
* EPS TTM ≈ **₹55–56** → real earnings, not accounting tricks
* ROE **18.8%**, ROCE **13.8%** → solid for asset-heavy ports
* Dividend yield exists (0.46%) → signals cash confidence

### What caps the score

* Market cap **₹3.3 lakh Cr** → size limits multi-bagger probability
* Valuation already prices in:

  * Volume growth
  * Margin stability
  * No funding shock
* Not cheap — just **fair for quality infra**

**Verdict:**
This is **not undervalued**, but also **not stretched** like Lemon Tree.

👉 **6.5/10**

---

## 2️⃣ SWOT / Business Quality Score — **8.5 / 10**

### Strengths (structural, not cyclical)

* **Near-monopoly economics** in Indian ports
* OPM consistently **55–63%** → insane pricing power for infra
* Cargo stickiness **~56%** → switching costs are real
* Operating cash flow **₹17,226 Cr (FY25)** → funding machine
* Integrated model (ports + logistics + marine) = moat deepening

### Weaknesses (real, but manageable)

* **Very high absolute debt** (₹56,851 Cr borrowings Sep’25)
* Interest cost rising again (₹484 → ₹1,223 quarterly)
* Other income volatility (can distort headline profit)

### Opportunities

* India trade + manufacturing + China+1 → secular tailwind
* Logistics & marine revenue scaling faster than ports
* International ports add optionality

### Threats (don’t ignore, but don’t overreact)

* Regulatory / political overhang (Adani-group specific)
* FX + global trade slowdown risk
* Capex-heavy — mistakes are expensive

**Verdict:**
This is **infrastructure with a moat**, not a commodity business.

👉 **8.5/10**

---

## 3️⃣ Sector Score (Ports & Logistics) — **7.5 / 10**

### Positives

* Long-duration assets
* High entry barriers (land, regulation, capital)
* Volumes track GDP + trade, not consumer whims

### Negatives

* Capital intensive forever
* Policy + geopolitical sensitivity
* Not a fast-growth sector — it’s a **cash-flow sector**

**Verdict:**
One of the **best infra sub-sectors** in India.

👉 **7.5/10**
